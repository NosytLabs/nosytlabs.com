## Materials Available

**PLA** - Best for gaming miniatures, decorative items
- Crisp details, multiple colors
- Easy to paint
- Biodegradable
- Print Temp: 200-220°C
- **Price**: Standard rate

**PETG** - Best for functional parts, durable items
- Stronger than PLA
- More durable
- Better for stress parts
- Print Temp: 230-250°C
- **Price**: +10% over PLA
