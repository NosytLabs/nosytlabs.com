# AI Integration Roadmap: Step-by-Step Implementation Guide for Business Owners

You know AI can help your business. But where do you start? Which tools? What's the timeline? How much will it cost?

This guide gives you a proven 6-month roadmap to integrate AI into your business successfully.

---

## AI Integration Framework

Before jumping into tools, understand this framework:

```
PLAN (Week 1-2)
↓
EXPERIMENT (Week 3-8)
↓
IMPLEMENT (Week 9-16)
↓
SCALE (Month 4-6)
↓
OPTIMIZE (Ongoing)
```

---

## Phase 1: PLAN (Weeks 1-2)

### 1.1 Identify High-Impact Opportunities

Not all tasks are worth automating. Focus on the biggest pain points.

**Questions to ask:**
- What takes the most time?
- What's repetitive and tedious?
- What requires manual data entry?
- What could be faster/cheaper?

**Typical candidates:**
✅ Customer support (FAQ, routine questions)
✅ Content creation (email, social, blogs)
✅ Data processing (invoices, forms, reports)
✅ Lead qualification (score and route)
✅ Report generation (data analysis, summaries)

❌ Creative work requiring human judgment
❌ Real-time decision-making under pressure
❌ Complex interpersonal communication
❌ Physical tasks

### 1.2 Estimate Potential Savings

For each high-impact task, calculate savings:

**Example: Customer Support**
- Current: 1 FTE support person × $50K/year
- Time on routine questions: 60%
- Potential with AI: Resolve 70% automatically
- **Savings: $50K × 60% × 70% = $21,000/year**

Typical savings:
- Support: $15K-$50K/year per person
- Content: $10K-$30K/year per person
- Data processing: $20K-$60K/year per person

## Phase 2: EXPERIMENT (Weeks 3-8)

### 2.1 Pilot with ChatGPT/Claude (Free or $20/month)

**Week 3-4: Proof of Concept**

Manually test if AI works for your use case:

**Support example:**
1. Copy 10 customer emails
2. Paste into ChatGPT with prompt
3. Read responses
4. Evaluate quality

**Time investment:** 2-4 hours
**Cost:** $0 (free tier) or $20 (Pro)
**Result:** Determine if AI can actually help

### 2.2 Run Small Experiments (Week 5-6)

**Pick 1-2 high-impact tasks**
**Run manual process + AI in parallel**
**Compare results**

**Example setup:**
```
Week 5: Manual process (baseline)
- Agent answers 50 customer emails
- Time: 5 hours
- Quality: Varies
- Cost: $125 (5 hours × $25/hr)

Week 6: AI-assisted process
- Agent uses ChatGPT to draft responses
- Time: 2 hours (editing + refinement)
- Quality: Higher, more consistent
- Cost: $2 (API usage) + $50 (labor)
- Total: $52
```

**Results:** 60% faster, 60% cheaper, better quality

### 2.3 Document What Works (Week 7-8)

**For each experiment, document:**
- What task did you automate?
- What was the result?
- Quality: Better? Same? Worse?
- Speed: How much faster?
- Cost: API cost + time saved = ROI?
