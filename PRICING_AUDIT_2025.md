## Key Considerations for Pricing

1. **No "Free" Offerings**
   - All services are paid, professional-grade
   - No free consultations, audits, or assessments in marketing

2. **Premium Positioning**
   - Prices reflect quality, expertise, and guaranteed ROI
   - Not competing on price - competing on value

3. **AI-Leverage Premium**
   - Services using Claude, GPT-4, and Cursor command premium rates
   - Fast delivery + quality = higher margins

4. **Transparent Communication**
   - Clear pricing on all services
   - No hidden fees or surprise costs
   - Professional estimates before engagement

## Competitive Advantage

- Transparent, honest pricing (no hidden costs)
- Market-rate or better rates
- AI-leveraged services at reasonable cost
- Premium quality at professional price points
- Fast delivery due to AI assistance
